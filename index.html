I am a Deputy Reseacher (equal to Associate Professor) at Laboratory of Intelligent Collaborative Computing, UESTC. Before working at UESTC, I earned my Ph.D. in Computer Science and Technology at UESTC in 2022 under the supervision of Prof. Heng Tao SHEN and co-supervision with Prof. Jie Shao. I obtained my BS degree in Software Engineering from UESTC in 2017. My research interests focus on computer vision and model compression, especially for lightweight deep image restoration, such as super-resolution, image deblurring and model quantization etc.
